Our Ventures

We are actively building new ventures to solve real-world problems. Here are some of our promising ideas in the works.

Audio Translation SaaS for Planetariums & Public Events

This is a SaaS platform that provides real-time audio translation for live presentations. It breaks down language barriers for diverse audiences in public venues like planetariums, museums, and conference halls.

Problem Solved

Content is often delivered in a single language, limiting engagement for tourists and non-native speakers. Existing solutions are expensive and inflexible.

Our Solution

A cloud-based platform that captures live audio and broadcasts real-time translations to a user's mobile device via a simple app.

Micro CRM for Community Businesses

This micro-SaaS product is designed to help small, local businesses in neighborhoods manage customer relationships, bookings, and communication effectively.

Problem Solved

Traditional CRM software is often too complex and expensive for small businesses. They need a simple, intuitive, and affordable tool.

Our Solution

A lightweight, user-friendly CRM with essential features like contact management, appointment scheduling, and automated reminders, tailored for local service providers.
